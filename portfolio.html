Step 1.

There once was an Electrical Engineer. This particular engineer enjoyed what he was doing - planning, designing and building. Well, kind of.

The peculiar thing is, he always had an itch. If you asked him he could never explain fully explain it. "All I know," he would say, "is that I want to do something else. Something: hands-on, faster-paced and exciting."

Step 2.

This engineer then remembered that once upon a time, before electrical engineering, he wanted to be something else - A programmer.

With this revelation in hand, he started to look for ways to make it happen. One month later, as quick as can be, he was back in school!

Step 3.

Armed with workable knowledge of several web development technologies, I began my new life as a coder.
